Vision vs IMU Motion Imitation

FYP | Sep 2025 – May 2026

Designed and evaluated two distinct imitation learning pipelines—vision-based and inertial-based—to train the Unitree G1 humanoid robot on human movements. The vision pipeline processes standard .mp4 videos using GVHMR framework for pose estimation, while the inertial pipeline utilizes raw motion capture data from ReboCap IMU sensors. Both modalities employ the General Motion Retargeting (GMR) framework for kinematic mapping and train parallelized policies within the MJLabs framework. The project culminates in a comprehensive comparative analysis evaluating the tradeoffs in tracking accuracy, training robustness, and efficiency between vision and IMU-driven data streams.

Velocity Keyboard Control

FYP | Sep 2025 – May 2026

Trained a Velocity control policy in MJLabs and developed custom control scripts to enable the robot to be controlled via the keyboard. Includes acceleration control and dynamic turning while in motion.

Reinforcement Learning

FYP | Sep 2025 – May 2026

Trained and simulate reinforcement learning policies for multiple actions on the Unitree G1 in IsaacGym by designing and tuning custom reward functions. Developed a push-resistant policy for a 29-DoF G1 humanoid through incremental learning and iterative optimisation of reward structures and training parameters.

Teleoperation

FYP | Sep 2025 – May 2026

Developed a teleoperation system for the Unitree G1 in MuJoCo using ReboCap IMU sensors for motion capture input, with remote communication enabled through ngrok for real-time control. The setup operated without physics simulation for direct motion replay, while also supporting integration of trained reinforcement learning policies to enable robot operation within physics-enabled environments.
